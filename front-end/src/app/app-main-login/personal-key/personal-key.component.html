Why do I need to store my new key on paper?

To protect your account, you need a password and access to your phone, or an authentication application at sign-in. If you can't use your phoen, you can sign in with your Personal Key instead. For your privacy and security, fec.gov does not store your password and Personal Key. Only you will know them. You will only have access and the ability to share your personal information. We urge you to store your Personal Key outside of your computer or mobile device so that it will be safe even if your devices are stolen or your online accounts are hacked. If you do not have your Personal Key and you forget your password, the way to keep your account safe is to verify that you are the legal owner by contacting the Electronic Filing Office at (202) 697-1307
